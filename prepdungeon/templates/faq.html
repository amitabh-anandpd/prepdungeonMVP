Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PrepDungeon and how it can transform your learning experience.

What is PrepDungeon?

PrepDungeon is an AI-powered personalized study platform that transforms any study material into a tailored learning experience. You can upload your syllabus or notes (PDF, DOC, links, etc.), and the platform's AI will analyze the content to create a custom study plan, quizzes, and feedback.

How does PrepDungeon personalize my learning?

PrepDungeon's AI algorithms track your performance in real-time, identifying knowledge gaps and adjusting lesson difficulty. It creates individualized learning paths based on your data and continuously personalizes exercises and recommendations, mimicking the effect of a private tutor.

What kind of study materials can I use with PrepDungeon?

Unlike fixed-course apps, PrepDungeon allows you to use your own syllabus or notes as input. This means any topic, textbook, or personal study goal can be the basis for PrepDungeon's tutoring.

How do adaptive assessments work on PrepDungeon?

Every quiz and practice test on PrepDungeon is adaptive. The platform dynamically adjusts question difficulty based on your past answers, offering harder questions for your strengths and simpler ones for weak topics. This helps guide you to what you need to learn next.

How does PrepDungeon make studying engaging?

PrepDungeon gamifies the learning process by turning studying into a game-like quest. You earn points and progress towards levels, complete daily quests, conquer "dungeons" (themed subject tests), and accumulate experience points (XP) and Curiosity Points, which can unlock fun features or content bonuses.

Can I track my progress with PrepDungeon?

Yes, PrepDungeon provides clear dashboards where you (and optionally your teachers or mentors) can monitor progress. It tracks quiz scores, topic proficiency, and overall XP/level, and identifies "weak topic alerts" (areas where scores are low) suggesting targeted revision.

How does PrepDungeon differ from other AI learning tools?

While some tools offer parts of PrepDungeon's features, PrepDungeon uniquely combines content-driven customization (using your actual syllabus) with a fully gamified experience and all-in-one learning journey.

What future enhancements are planned for PrepDungeon?

After the initial release (MVP), planned enhancements include multiple test modes (Conceptual, Speed), an adaptive testing engine that adjusts question difficulty, more extensive gamification features (points, levels, badges, quests), personalized study plans and tracking, social and competitive features (leaderboards, study groups), and potentially a mobile app or integrations with existing LMS tools.

What are "Weakness Alerts" and "Focus Mode" in PrepDungeon?

"Weakness Alerts" pinpoint specific concepts where your scores are low, flagging them for review (e.g., "Calculus: Integration - 62% correct"). "Focus Mode" includes a built-in Pomodoro-style timer and smart tips to remind you when to study or which topics to tackle next.

Is PrepDungeon free to use?

Yes! PrepDungeon offers a generous free tier that includes basic screening tests, progress tracking, and limited AI recommendations.

Do I need to sign up to try PrepDungeon?

No, you can start a screening test without creating an account or sharing personal data, lowering the barrier to use and allowing you to try the tool instantly. You will be prompted to create an account after the quiz if you wish to save your results.
