Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PrepDungeon and how it can transform your learning experience.

How do I get started with PrepDungeon?

Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for a free account, upload your study materials or take our initial screening test, and our AI will create a personalized learning plan for you. You can start with any subject and begin taking tests immediately.

Do I need to upload study materials to use PrepDungeon?

No, uploading study materials is optional. While uploading your materials helps us create more personalized content, you can start using PrepDungeon immediately with our pre-built question banks covering various subjects like Mathematics, Computer Science, Physics, and more.

How does the AI personalization work?

Our AI analyzes your performance patterns, learning speed, strengths, and weaknesses to create a unique learning profile. It then adapts question difficulty, suggests study topics, and creates personalized study schedules that optimize your learning efficiency.

What types of tests are available?

We offer three main test types: MCQ (Multiple Choice Questions) for quick assessment, Conceptual tests for deep understanding, and Speed tests for rapid recall. Each test type is designed to evaluate different aspects of your knowledge and learning style.

How does the gamification system work?

You earn XP (Experience Points) for completing tests and study sessions, maintain daily streaks, unlock achievement badges, and level up as you progress. You can also compete with friends on leaderboards and participate in weekly challenges to stay motivated.

Is PrepDungeon free to use?

Yes! PrepDungeon offers a generous free tier that includes basic screening tests, progress tracking, and limited AI recommendations. Premium features like unlimited tests, advanced analytics, and priority support are available with our paid plans.

Can I change my account information?

Yes, you can update your profile information, academic details, study preferences, and notification settings anytime from your profile page. Some changes like email updates may require verification for security purposes.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the account settings page. Please note that this action is irreversible and will permanently remove all your data, progress, and achievements. We recommend downloading your data before deletion if needed.

What devices and browsers are supported?

PrepDungeon works on all modern devices including desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. We support the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge browsers. For the best experience, we recommend using Chrome or Firefox.

Do I need an internet connection to use PrepDungeon?

Yes, PrepDungeon requires an internet connection as it's a web-based platform. However, we're working on offline capabilities for certain features like downloaded study materials and practice tests.

I'm experiencing technical issues. What should I do?

First, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the issue persists, please contact our support team through the contact page with details about the problem, your browser, and device information. We typically respond within 4 hours for technical issues.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and various local payment methods depending on your region. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period, after which your account will revert to the free tier.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! We offer special student pricing with up to 50% off our premium plans. You'll need to verify your student status with a valid .edu email address or student ID. Contact our support team for more details about student discounts.
